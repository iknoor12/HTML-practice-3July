Wikipedia-style Article

This is a simple Wikipedia-style article.

Introduction

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia that allows users to edit and contribute articles on a wide range of topics. It is one of the most popular reference websites on the internet.

History

Wikipedia was launched on January 15, 2001, by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger. It has since grown to become one of the largest and most comprehensive sources of information available online.

Content

Wikipedia articles are written collaboratively by volunteers around the world. The content is available in multiple languages and covers a vast array of subjects, including history, science, culture, and more
